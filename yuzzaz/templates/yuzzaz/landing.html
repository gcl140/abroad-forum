Why we built TZtoAbroad

Hey fam 🌍✨ — after two gap years of navigating confusing deadlines, hidden scholarships, and a lot of silence, I realized how isolating the journey to studying abroad can feel. Just before I left Tanzania myself, I thought: what if there was a space where we could ask, share, and guide each other through this process? That’s how TZtoAbroad was born 💡.

This isn’t just my story — it’s for every student still searching for the right info, the right chance, the right push. While Tansaf gave us an incredible platform, so many others are still out there without guidance. If we can help even one more person find their path, it’s worth it 🕊️.
