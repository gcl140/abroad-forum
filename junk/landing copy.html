Why we built this

Gift and Benny launched ForumAbroad because they saw the same pattern: talented students misled by outdated advice, missing deadlines, and applying without mentorship. They wanted a portable, trusted space where every piece of guidance is backed by someone who has been through the process.

1
Reduce uncertainty
Step-by-step checklists and timeline visualizations for each application path.
2
Amplify trust
Alumni-verified threads and mentor badges so you can follow evidence, not hearsay.
3
Scale support
AI tooling for drafts combined with human review for nuance and accuracy.
