Posting Guidelines

  1. Stick to the topic — studying abroad, applications, scholarships, visas, etc.
  2. Be clear and specific — include details like deadlines, programs, or your background.
  3. No spam, ads, or irrelevant links — this space is for real help only.
  4. Be kind and respectful — everyone here is figuring things out.
  5. Check if your question already exists — use the search bar first.
  6. Write helpful titles — avoid vague stuff like “Help plz.”
  7. Share verified info — or ask clearly if you're unsure about something.
  8. If it’s personal (e.g. finances or rejection letters), feel free to go anonymous.
